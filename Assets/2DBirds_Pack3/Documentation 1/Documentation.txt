	
--------------2D Birds Pack3----------------------------

This package contains birds spritesheet animations for 2D Games.

Technical Details:-

		- Resolution : 4000 * 2504
		- Spritesheets : 16
		- Frame rate : 25
		
Features:-

	- 5 Birds and their varients
	- Simple cartoon stylized birds
	- Easy to change colors
	- Prefabs included
		
How to use:-
	
	Simply drag and drop the "Birds" prefab to your scene and use it.
	(Prefab(s) located under 
	Asset > Birds Pack3 > Prefabs).
